Running ELYXIER without touching a line of code

Your site's words, photos, and prices now live in a simple dashboard called Sanity Studio — separate from the website itself, so you can update it any time without waiting on us.

Step One

Logging In

Your Studio lives right on your site, at:

elyxier.vercel.app/studio

You'll get an email invite from Sanity at the address we have on file. Open it, pick a sign-in method (Google is easiest), and you're in. Bookmark the link above — that's your permanent front door.

Step Two

What You Can Edit

The Studio sidebar is split into two groups: Page Content (the wording and images around the site) and Products / Testimonials (things you'll add to and reorder more often).

Hero
The big opening headline, subtext, and background photo — the very first thing a visitor sees.
Marquee Ticker
The scrolling gold banner of words ("Whipped · Handmade · Skin Ritual...") just under the hero.
Products
Each body butter's name, price, description, and photos. Reorder them with the sort number — lower shows first.
Brand Story (About)
Your story section — the paragraphs about why ELYXIER exists, plus the pull-quote.
Live Selling CTA
The "Shop With Us Live" section and its TikTok / Instagram / Facebook / Whatnot buttons.
Testimonials
Customer quotes shown in the reviews section.
Community Sign-up
The email-capture section's headline, the three benefit cards, and the fine print.
Site Settings
Your logo, nav menu labels, footer text, and social links — the things that appear on every page.
Before you launch
Two products — Rose Petal Silk and Citrus Burst — are currently showing stock stand-in photos, not your own. Swap those in the Products section whenever your real photos are ready.
Step Three

Going Live

Hit Publish on anything you change and it appears on elyxier.vercel.app within a few seconds — no waiting, no re-deploying, nothing else to do.

When You're Ready

Connecting Shopify

Right now, "Add to Ritual" is a display-only button — there's no checkout wired up yet. When you're ready to actually sell, here's the path:

  1. Open a Shopify store

    Add your six products there with the same names and photos you're using in the Studio, so everything matches across TikTok Shop, Amazon, and your site.

  2. Pick how deep you want to go

    Two options below — talk to us if you're not sure which fits.

Simplest

Shopify Buy Button

A small "buy" snippet Shopify generates for each product, dropped into the site. Fastest and cheapest to set up — checkout looks and feels like Shopify's, not fully custom.

Full Custom

Headless Checkout

Cart and checkout built to match your site's exact look, powered behind the scenes by Shopify. More capable, but it's a real build — this one's on us, not a DIY step.

One More Thing

Your Domain

You mentioned having a GoDaddy account — that's fine to keep exactly as is. Your site needs to stay hosted where it is now (Vercel); GoDaddy just needs to point elyxier.com at it, a quick DNS change we can walk you through when you're ready to switch off the .vercel.app address.
